About Us

Who Are We

GIT is a Ugandan registered private limited liability company incorporated in 2009 Has operated with offices in Gulu and Kampala We have been involved in providing construction materials, engineering consultancy services, ICT services to several customers GIT has now enhanced its business with a key focus on providing construction materials and services The main area of operation of GIT will be Northern Uganda with Head Office at Koro Abilli, near Gulu

Why We Exist

Northern Uganda is undergoing a reconstruction after 20 years of war Infrastructure rollout is a top government priority Availability of land is attracting many investors Many diaspora people are coming back home This has created a great need of construction materials and services There is limited supply of high quality construction materials and services in Northern Uganda GIT construction materials is the solution to the problem
